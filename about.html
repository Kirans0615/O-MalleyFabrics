Grainne O'Malley

My name is Grainne O'Malley, I grew up sewing and helping my parents in the family fabric store in Ireland. I had the privilege of learning the craft from the very best – my mother.

For decades, my mother has served her community and generations of customers. Growing up amidst the whir of sewing machines and the comforting scent of fresh textiles, I absorbed not just the technical skills, but also a commitment to quality and deep connection to every customers.

With O'Malley Fabrics, my aim is to carry on that beautiful legacy. Whether you need a simple alteration to make an outfit feel just right, custom curtains to brighten your home, or sewing lessons to learn the trade. I approach every task with the same dedication and precision passed down through generations.

It would be my pleasure to assist you with your sewing needs, big or small, and share a piece of my heritage with you.
