Grainne O'Malley: Carrying on a Rich Tradition

Discover the passion and heritage behind every stitch at O'Malley Fabrics.

Grainne O'Malley

Dia dhuit! My name is **Grainne O'Malley**, and I'm delighted to bring the spirit of traditional Irish sewing to my new home here in the **DMV area**. My journey in fabric and thread began long ago in a vibrant shop in **County Mayo, Ireland**. There, I had the privilege of learning the craft from the very best – my mother.

For decades, my mother has served her community with meticulous care, transforming fabrics into cherished items. Growing up amidst the whir of her sewing machine and the comforting scent of fresh textiles, I absorbed not just the technical skills, but also her unwavering commitment to quality and her deep connection to her customers.

With O'Malley Fabrics, my aim is to carry on that beautiful legacy. Whether you need a simple alteration to make an outfit feel just right, custom curtains to brighten your home, or a beloved armchair given new life through reupholstery, I approach every task with the same dedication and precision passed down through generations.

I believe that well-made items bring lasting joy and comfort. It would be my pleasure to assist you with your sewing needs, big or small, and share a piece of my heritage with you. Thank you for welcoming O'Malley Fabrics into your community!
